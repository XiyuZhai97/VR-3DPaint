right controller
- hold trigger to draw
- swipe right-to-left to undo 1 stroke
- swipe down-to-up to increase stroke size
- swipe up-to-down to decrease stroke size
- hold down grip and drag side-to-side to rotate sketch
- double-click touchpad to toggle teleportation mode
- in teleportation mode, click trigger to teleport to teleport sprite

left controller
- click trigger to select color by saturation/value
- click/drag on hue bar w/ trigger held down to change hue
- point at sphere on lower left (it should turn blue) and click trackpad to reset

both controllers
- hold both controller grips and move hands apart/together to scale sketch
